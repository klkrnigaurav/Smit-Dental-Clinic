Common Dental Concerns We Help With:

I'm Concern About Safety

We realize that now more than ever safety is a concern. We built a state of the art office during the pandemic which allowed us to take even more precautions. In our clinic you will see:

  • Full-height walls between treatment rooms.
  • A spacious office that allows for social distancing in non-treatment areas.
  • Upgraded high-efficiency particulate air filters to improve room filtration which reduces transmission of airborne particles.
  • Location in a medical office building completed in 2020 which boasts hospital-grade air exchange rates for office air circulation.
  • The latest technologies implemented for equipment cleaning & sanitation.

I Have Tooth Pain

This happens to the best of us. If you’re experience discomfort or high sensitivity it’s best to contact us right away. Waiting can lead to more complicated issues down the road. In most cases, we can get you in the same day and make you comfortable.

Typical Results: You get out of pain and now have a dentist you trust that won’t judge you.

I’m Embarrassed That I Haven’t Been To The Dentist In A While

I’m embarrassed that I haven’t had my oil changed this year! No need to feel embarrassed. It is true that the longer you delay dental treatment the more painful and expensive it becomes. I won’t judge you if you don’t judge me.

Typical Results: You find a dentist that can provide you consistent care and a healthy mouth without judging you.

I’m Ready For A Smile I Want To Show Off :

There is no need to keep your smile hidden. I love helping people feel confident in showing their teeth. This journey is about you and the ones you love. I want to make sure you smile along the way.

Typical Results: I love doing this work because it’s transformational. There are numerous cosmetic options that will get you the smile you want.

What Are Our Specialities !

Preventative Dental Care

Your oral health is very important to us and we will strive to make it as easy as possible for you to receive the best dental care. During your cleaning and wellness screening, Dr. Kulkarni will review your medical and dental health history and any questions or concerns you may have to gather a complete picture of your current oral health.

Invisalign® & Orthodontics

Invisalign clear aligners or traditional wire braces are available to straighten your teeth and bite. We offer a FREE 3D scan and consultation to our current patients.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is one of the most popular cosmetic treatments in our office. Teeth Whitening is a quick and effective way to gain confidence in your smile. We have several options.
