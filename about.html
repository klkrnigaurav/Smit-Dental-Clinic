Dr. Ganesh Kulkarni's Smit Dental Clinic

Smit Dental Clinic is dedicated to providing exceptional dental care to all our patients. We always strive for delivering the best results possible, and provide the type of service that makes our patients smile! We offer affordable, quality implant, cosmetic dentistry services & children’s dentistry in our state-of-the-art clinic.

Our goal remains to fulfill our patients’ expectations and dreams of having a healthy, beautiful smile; and we deliver results by providing exceptional, personalized care.

Smit Dental Clinic is committed to change the way people feel about dentistry. We believe that going to the dentist does not need to be painful or traumatic. We make our patients feel at home and offer them unmatched dental treatment. As a result, our patients feel comfortable at our clinic and, after seeing the results of their treatment, they return home happier than when they arrived.

We do whatever is within our power to ensure a great dental tourism experience for our patients, and make sure each and every patient leaves Ansh Dental satisfied and smiling brightly.

Our specialty is full mouth rehabilitation, Implant Dentistry & Dentistry for Kids and our focus is on long-term dental restorations using the best materials & techniques.
